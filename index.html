About

Welcome to Jigster: Jigsaw Puzzles, a sliding tile puzzle game that’ll really test how well you can think spatially and recognize patterns — we’ve got hundreds of puzzles that look great too.

  • Start simple.

  • Think deeper.

How To Play

Classic Sliding Puzzle Mechanics

  • Start with shuffled puzzle pieces arranged in a grid.

  • Slide tiles into adjacent empty spaces to rearrange them.

  • Reconstruct the complete image piece by piece.

  • Solve the puzzle to unlock the next level.

Sounds simple? Think again. As grids grow larger and images become more complex, every move counts.
